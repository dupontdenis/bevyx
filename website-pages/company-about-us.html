About Bevyx

Software is pushing the world's progress unlike anything else, making every company a software company. Cloud Computing has been driving this progress in the past decade by commoditizing compute infrastructure. We believe that Cloud-Native, which is a layer of technologies complementing the Cloud Computing concept, is next and so our mission at Bevyx is to push the Cloud Native stack further and to make it accessible to everyone.
At Bevyx, we see a world where every company can have an advanced infrastructure based on cutting edge technologies affordably.
