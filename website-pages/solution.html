Canary Releases

Decision making by data-driven experiments is crucial to ensure your company's competitive advantage. Canary Releases helps you achieve A/B Testing in an easy and effective way.

Remesh brings a straightforward approach to achieve Canary Releases for your complex microservices environment.




Works with your application
Don't need to make code changes

Controlled Testing in Production
Run your routine tests in your production easily and safely

Testing in Production

The production is becoming ever so valuable for test purposes, while at the same time simulating your production is becoming more complex.

Remesh makes it possible to safely and confidently run your tests on the production and gradually test your releases with real traffic as you take a release from zero to hero.
Remesh is changing the way we think about testing, making it a routine process that is integral to your product.
